◆ Priority — Investigation Required
High-Speed Stability Concern

During initial testing, Grotti Engineering has flagged an unresolved high-speed stability issue with the prototype. At sustained speeds above 160 KM/H, drivers have reported the rear end feeling light and unsettled — particularly through long sweeping corners and under hard braking. There appears to be insufficient downforce on the rear axle, causing the back end to lose traction unpredictably. The root cause has not yet been determined and may relate to the current rear bodywork configuration. We need the test team's input to help identify what's causing the issue and what modification might resolve it.

Reported Symptom
Rear-End Lift at Speed

Above 160 KM/H the rear feels light and floaty. The back of the car appears to be generating lift rather than being pushed down onto the road surface.

Reported Symptom
Oversteer Under Braking

Hard braking from high speed causes the rear to step out. The back of the car doesn't seem to have enough aerodynamic load keeping it planted during deceleration.

Reported Symptom
Cornering Instability

Through fast corners the rear slides more than expected. Airflow over the rear deck may not be managing downforce effectively in the current configuration.

LGL.001 — Binding Terms
Validation Contract Terms

The following terms govern the Factory Test Team agreement between Grotti Auto Group and Redline Motorsports.

§1 Redline Motorsports ("Test Team") shall accumulate a minimum of 2,500 validated kilometres on the designated prototype within the testing window.
§2 All telemetry sessions must be logged via the Prototype Validation Portal. Incomplete or unsigned entries will not count toward the kilometre requirement.
§3 A minimum of three (3) stability feedback reports must be submitted with severity and grip ratings regarding the high-speed handling concern.
§4 Upon successful completion of the validation kilometres, data submission requirements, and showcase obligations, full ownership of the prototype shall be transferred to Redline Motorsports, free and clear of any liens or encumbrances.
